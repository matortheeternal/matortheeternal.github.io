About me

Hello! I am Colin Allen, a 22-year-old software developer from Goleta, California. Online, I go by the alias "Mator", which is pronounced "Mah-tore".

I have been building software for over 3 years now, and I am a very passionate developer. I learned to program early 2013 when I wanted to make a Skyrim mod and realized I could save a lot of time by automating it. Since then I have participated and won several hackathons, developed various projects, and learned over a dozen programming languages. Today, I live and breathe software.

I am greatly motivated by the ways in which software can help people. Good software can save people a lot of time and effort, and allow them to accomplish things that would not otherwise be possible. I like knowing that the software I have built has made some impact on people's lives.

Beyond Software

Beyond the realm of software development, I enjoy cooking, teaching, backpacking, creative writing, watching anime, listening to music, playing video games, and spending time with my cats.

I cook all my own meals using fresh ingredients, and have a persistent obsession with pasta. I can cook over a dozen different sauces, and usually eat pasta at least 3 times a week. I don't eat candy, cake, ice cream, or soft drinks. Weird, huh? :P

I've tutored both math and computer science. I really enjoy teaching people, and feel that describing things and processes comes second nature to me. Unlike many software developers, I actually enjoy writing documentation. (no, really!)
