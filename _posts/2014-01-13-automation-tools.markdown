Automation Tools for TES5Edit is a collection of scripts for automating tasks 
that are commonly performed when editing ESP files in 
[TES5Edit](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/25859).  Automation Tools has 
over 1,000 endorsements on Nexus Mods and has been downloaded by over 6,000 
unique users.

This is one of my favorite projects because it basically allowed me the freedom 
to make any script I thought could save people time when creating mods.  These 
scripts have saved my users thousands of hours of time, and have been 
invaluable in the creation of several Skyrim mods.

## Development Status
Automation Tools development is currently on hold until I can complete my 
mteFunctions refactor.
